About Us
VIT Bhopal University
VIT Bhopal University, envisioned with a new global outlook aims at empowering its students to attain excellence through learning. Located at the heart of India, it is led by our Founder and Honorable Chancellor Dr. G Viswanathan. It is the first private university in India with 100% Doctoral faculty. CALTech is our method of bringing in all-round development in our students. Along with this, various technical,nontechnical and cultural clubs enhance the social and creative skills of our students.
